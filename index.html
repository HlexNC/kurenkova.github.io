Apple Pie Recipe

Ingredients

1 tablespoon granulated sugar
200 g of softened butter
200 g of sour cream
1 egg
Flour (approximately 13-15 tablespoons)
6 round apples (preferably sweet and soft)
6 tablespoons granulated sugar (for sprinkling on the apples)

Preparation

1. Mix sugar, butter, sour cream, and egg.
2. Gradually add flour.
3. Place the dough on cling film and shape it into a round ball or a long brick.
4. Peel the apples and grate them coarsely, squeeze out the juice.
5. Divide the dough into two parts. Roll out one part and place it on a baking sheet.
6. Lay the grated apples on top of the dough and sprinkle them with 6 tablespoons of sugar.
7. Cover the apples with the second part of the dough, pinch the edges. Make several holes in the top part of the dough with a fork and one round hole in the center.
8. Bake at 180°C (356°F) for approximately 25-30 minutes.
